About

I'm a postdoctoral research fellow at The University of Texas at Austin, in the Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences. Specifically, I am part of the PECOS group.

If you'd like to see my CV you can go here.


The Predictive Engineering and Computational Science (PECOS) group largely investigates uncertainty in large systems of nonlinear equations. One of our principal goals is to understand the effect of incorporating noisy observations into uncertain PDEs.

Software
